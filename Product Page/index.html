At Eau de Livre, we have redefined the art of fragrance by merging the timeless allure of literature with the world of fine perfumery. Our brand is a celebration of the written word and the evocative power of scent, where every bottle tells a unique olfactory story inspired by the pages of beloved books.

Why Eau de Livre can be the best choice for you?

Crafted with Passion

Our master perfumers are not just artisans; they are avid bookworms who understand the depth of emotion and imagery that words can conjure. They meticulously blend the finest ingredients from around the world to capture the essence of the literary worlds that have captured your heart.

Unforgettable Fragrances

Each of our fragrances is a chapter waiting to be experienced. From the intoxicating bouquet of a Victorian garden to the smoky intrigue of a noir detective novel, our scents transport you to the heart of your favorite stories. Whether you seek the romanticism of classic literature or the excitement of contemporary fiction, NovelScents has a fragrance that resonates with your literary tastes.

Sustainability & Quality

We take pride in our commitment to sustainability and quality. Our ingredients are ethically sourced, and our packaging is eco-friendly. We believe in a world where beauty and responsibility coexist harmoniously.

A Personal Journey

Eau de Livre is not just a brand; it's an invitation to embark on a personal olfactory journey through the world of literature. Choose a scent that resonates with your favorite books or explore new narratives through our carefully curated collection.

Up and coming: Scented Storybook Collections

At NovelScents, we're constantly exploring innovative ways to enhance your experience of literature-inspired fragrances. Introducing our new feature: "Scented Storybook Collections."

Imagine opening a beautifully crafted box to find not only a bottle of our exquisite perfume but also a small, elegant book. This book isn't just any book; it's a scented storybook specially designed to accompany your fragrance. Each collection is curated to match the fragrance with a literary masterpiece, creating a multi-sensory journey through the pages and scents of a beloved book.
